Makeup Artist . Educator . Enterpreneur

Lovingly called the "NITU- The Artist"

Known affectionately as the "Queen of Makeup" within the industry and by her devoted fans, Nitu has forged a remarkable journey from her origins as a lawyer to becoming one of Jharkhand's most prominent makeup artists. Today, her influence spans across social media platforms with approximately 2000 followers and continues to grow exponentially on Instagram, Facebook, and YouTube. Long before receiving her parents' approval, Kumari Nitu Rani was already experimenting with the boldest lip colors and trendiest nail paints. where she would skillfully transform friends and relatives. This early passion for enhancing natural beauty paved the way for her transition into professional makeup artistry.
